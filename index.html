About AnythingHub

The ultimate public directory for discovering the best websites, tools, and resources on the internet.

What is AnythingHub?

AnythingHub is a curated, community-driven directory of the web's most useful, interesting, and powerful websites. From AI tools and developer utilities to learning platforms and creative resources — we've catalogued them all.

Think of us as a combination of Product Hunt, FMHY, and a modern bookmark manager, rebuilt from the ground up for discoverability and ease of use.

Our Mission

The internet is vast. Finding the right tool at the right time is hard. AnythingHub exists to cut through the noise and surface genuinely useful websites across every category of human interest.

Categories We Cover

  • 🤖 AI & Creativity — AI tools, generators, and creative platforms
  • 📚 Learning & Education — Courses, tutorials, and educational tools
  • 🔬 Research & Knowledge — Academic resources, wikis, archives
  • 💻 Developer Tools — Code tools, APIs, utilities for developers
  • 🎨 Design & Creative Tools — Design software, mockup tools, assets
  • 🎮 Gaming — Online games, gaming utilities, emulators
  • 🎬 Entertainment — Streaming, anime, media platforms
  • 💪 Fitness — Workout planners, health trackers
  • ⚡ Productivity — Task managers, notes, automation tools
  • 🔧 Utilities — File tools, converters, online utilities
  • 🧪 Science — Science simulators, interactive labs
  • 🎵 Music — Music learning, creation, and streaming
  • 🏠 Home Design — Floor planners, interior design tools
  • 💼 Career & Freelancing — Job boards, freelance platforms
